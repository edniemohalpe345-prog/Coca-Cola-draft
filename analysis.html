Coca-Cola Website Analysis

Current Website Analysis

Strengths:

  • Strong branding (iconic red and white identity).
  • Clear visuals that evoke happiness and refreshment.
  • Global recognition and trust.

Weaknesses:

  • Too much information on the original homepage (cluttered).
  • Navigation could be overwhelming for new visitors.
  • Previous versions had slower loading due to heavy images/CSS.

Improvements (Implemented in this pure HTML version):

  • Simpler navigation
  • Faster loading speed
  • Organized content
  • KPI tracking built-in

Website Goals & Objectives

  1. Promote Coca-Cola products → Product page with 7+ items.
  2. Increase brand awareness → History, mission, values included.
  3. Provide product information → Detailed descriptions, sizes, best-for notes.
  4. Increase online engagement → Contact form, social links, product click tracking.
